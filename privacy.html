Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

The Short Version

RideReady does not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any of your personal data. Your ride templates, checklists, and history belong entirely to you.

Data Storage

All data you create in RideReady — including ride templates, checklist items, categories, and ride history — is stored in one of two places:

  • Locally on your device
  • In your personal, private iCloud account (if you choose to enable iCloud sync)

The developer has no ability to access, view, or retrieve any data stored on your device or in your iCloud account. Your data is yours alone.

No Data Collection

RideReady does not collect any information about you or your usage of the app. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • No third-party analytics SDKs or tracking frameworks
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No crash reporting that includes personal data

No Account Required

RideReady does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or sign in with any service. The app works fully without any form of registration or identification.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit framework. This sync happens directly between your devices through Apple's infrastructure. The developer does not operate any servers and has no access to your iCloud data.

You can disable iCloud sync at any time in the app's settings. When disabled, all data is stored locally on your device only.

In-App Purchases

RideReady Pro is available as an in-app purchase through Apple's App Store. All purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple. The developer does not receive or store any payment information, billing details, or financial data.

Third-Party Services

RideReady does not integrate with, send data to, or receive data from any third-party services, APIs, or servers. The app operates entirely on-device and through Apple's iCloud infrastructure.

Children's Privacy

Because RideReady does not collect any data from any user, it does not knowingly or unknowingly collect data from children under 13 or any other age group.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date. Because RideReady does not collect contact information, we cannot notify users directly — please check this page periodically.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or about RideReady, you can reach us at:

support@ridereadycycling.com
